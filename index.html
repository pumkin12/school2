What's New

  • COVID-19 Vaccination Camp at IVWS

    On 8th and 9th June 2021, a Covid – 19 vaccination camp has been set up in the school premises in collaboration with the Rotary Club of Kolkata Urbana and AMRI Hospitals. The camp was inaugurated by the Rotary District Governor, Mr Sudip Mukherjee and the District Governor Elect, Mr Prabir Chatterjee along with Rotary Club of Kolkata Urbana’s President Mr Arindam Sil and Secretary, Jayanta Guha. Eminent film personalities Devlina Kumar and Sauraseni Maitra were present on this occasion to support the initiative. The Director of IVWS Mrs. Amita Prasad spoke about the strong belief that the school has in serving the community and the importance of such collaborations. The camp has received an overwhelming response and approximately 600 people will be vaccinated on Day 1.

  • ONLINE CLASSES AT IVWS DURING LOCKDOWN

    The country is facing the fear of the Covid 19 pandemic due to which from midnight 22 March we are facing a lockdown situation. However we have decided to keep the spirit of our students up by beginning the new session on time, ‘ ONLINE!’ Our students are studying very hard with their teachers through live classes being conducted every day as per their timetable. Classes are being conducted for all grades everyday and the teachers and students are truly enjoying this new experience.




Community Outreach

In order to inculcate a sense of compassion and a spirit of giving to the less fortunate, IVWS has entered into partnerships with ‘Ektara’ (an NGO located in Topsia), Samaritan Help Mission ( located in Tikiapara) and ‘Premasree’ (a free residential school for the blind) Students of Ektara visit Indus Valley World School every week for sports and activities. We are working with the Samaritan Help Mission to enhance their teacher development programme. Students are also actively engaged in cleaning the school, neighbourhood and raising awareness of the need to maintain hygiene.
